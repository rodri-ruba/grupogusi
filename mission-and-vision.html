Us

Mission

Producing, processing, and commercializing bovine and derived meat products with quality and competitive prices for convenience stores and final consumption in accordance with international and national norms, achieving timely and successful delivery.

Vision

To achieve the first place in highest quality in international and national meat products.

Values

1. Honesty
Being transparent and upright in all our actions.
2. Respect
Maintaining an environment of mutual respect with our colleagues, clients, and suppliers that contributes to building healthy and productive relationships.
3. Commitment to our country
With our work we are contributing to the prosperity of our community and mexican society.
4. Discipline
Being consistent with the compliance of our work, fulfilling the established goals in an organized manner.
5. Responsibility
Through our daily work, we reaffirm the commitment with ourselves and with grupo gusi, with its growth and development.
6. Teamwork
Teamwork strengthens our human quality and sense of belonging allowing us to be more productive in the achievement of our objectives and goals.
